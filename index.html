Building from curiosity

I'm Julius Ayodeji, a self-taught full stack developer from Ibadan, Nigeria.

I started coding at age 15 out of curiosity and a love for building things. What began as a fascination with how websites worked turned into a relentless drive to ship products that matter.

Today, I'm building Payric — a finance SaaS designed specifically for Nigerian small businesses and freelancers.

I believe in shipping fast, building in public, and creating products that genuinely help people.
